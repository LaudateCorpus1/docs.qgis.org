**********************************************
Procedure for the management of sponsorships
**********************************************

* first contact could be made to anyone, within the PSC or outside
* the potential or prospective sponsor should be addressed to the sponsorship manager
* after successful negotiation, we check the payment has arrived
* we add it to the sponsorship page, with the expiring date, in the appropriate order
* we also add it to the banner, and wherever appropriate
* we send a confirmation / thank you letter
* when appropriate, We will do some social media promotion.
